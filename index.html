Ghats along the Ganges: Varanasi is famous for its ghats, which are series of steps leading down to the river. The ghats are bustling with activity throughout the day, offering a mesmerizing view of people performing various rituals, religious ceremonies, and bathing in the holy river.

Tucked away in the heart of Varanasi, one of the oldest and holiest cities in India, lies a charming restaurant that captures the essence of the city's rich culinary traditions.
